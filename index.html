Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

What documents are needed for ITR filing? ▼

You need: PAN card, Aadhaar card, Form 16 (from employer), bank statements, investment proofs (80C, 80D), and details of any other income. We guide you through the exact list based on your situation.

When should a business register for GST? ▼

GST registration is mandatory if annual turnover exceeds ₹40 lakhs (goods) or ₹20 lakhs (services). Also needed for inter-state supply, e-commerce sellers, and reverse charge cases.

How much does ITR filing cost? ▼

Our ITR filing charges depend on complexity — salary income starts from ₹499. Please contact us for an exact quote based on your income sources and filing type.

Can you handle GST notices and scrutiny? ▼

Yes, we have extensive experience handling GST notices, demand orders, and departmental scrutiny. We prepare proper replies and represent you before GST authorities.

How long does company registration take? ▼

We handle the complete process from name approval (MCA) to Certificate of Incorporation. You need basic KYC of directors and a registered office address. Process takes 7–10 working days.

Do you serve clients outside Patna? ▼

Yes! Most services like ITR, GST filing, and company registration can be done fully online. We serve clients across Bihar and all over India via email, WhatsApp and phone.
